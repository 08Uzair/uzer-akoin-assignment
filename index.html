About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated environments.

Through collaboration with UK universities and industry projects, IDR bridges the gap between academic research and real-world implementation.

Our Model

Academy

Bootcamps and professional training programs designed to develop practical expertise in digital risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new risk frameworks, governance models, and AI oversight methodologies.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement frameworks such as NIST, ISO and technology risk strategies.
